Voty

is an e-vote solution based on blockchain technologie, developed using Hyperledger fabric framework as PFA project at INSAT,Tunisia

How it works ?
1 | Admin enrollment

Newtork admin create an account for election 'creator', called also election admin

2 | Election creation

That election admin use his account (sent to mail) to create election + adding voters (providing their e-mail addresses)

3 | Voting

Every voter can login using his credentiels (sent to his mail) and become a candidate during the candidacy phase / vote during voting phase (he can do both)

4 | Results

After the end of the election phase, results become accessible

The key features

Integrity

All intractions are translated to blockchain transaction which grant a high degree of persistance

Unlinkability

each vote will be recorded, but with no link to the voter

Decentralization

the elections related actions doesn't require a third trusted tier to take place, which give more freedom and flexibility to the users
